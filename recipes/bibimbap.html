Bibimbap

About the dish

Bibimbap - "bibida" to mix, "bap" - rice/meal. It's delicious, filling and packed with veggies. Geat for your physical and mental health overall.

Ingredients

  • Veggies
  • Lean cut of beef
  • Cooked rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Gochujang
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Egg

Recipie

  1. Cook your beef
  2. Cook your veggies
  3. Assemble everything
  4. Put fried egg and gochujang on top
  5. Enjoy!
